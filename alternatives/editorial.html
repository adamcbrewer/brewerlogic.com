Section 01

About

Former British Army aircraft technician turned web developer. I spent five years maintaining helicopters before moving into tech full-time in 2010. It was a leap, but the discipline and precision I learned in the military translated directly into how I approach engineering problems today.

I love building component systems, interfaces and APIs. Communication is one of my strongest assets—I'm comfortable translating between frontend teams, backend engineers and stakeholders. I've found that the best code comes from understanding the people who will use it.

“The discipline and precision I learned in the military translated directly into how I approach engineering”

I've worked with brands including Google, Apple, The Economist, Burberry, Adidas and Virgin Galactic. In 2019, I co-founded Paper Trails with my designer brother Jake—a SaaS platform that turns adventure data into wall art.

Outside of work, I spend time with my wife, two girls and our Vizsla. I run, cycle, lift heavy things and build furniture.
