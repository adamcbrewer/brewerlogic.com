The Artist

Former British Army aircraft technician turned web developer. I spent five years maintaining helicopters before moving into tech full-time in 2010. It was a leap, but the discipline and precision I learned in the military translated directly into how I approach engineering problems today.

I love building component systems, interfaces and APIs. Communication is one of my strongest assets—I'm comfortable translating between frontend teams, backend engineers and stakeholders, and getting stuck in wherever needed. The best code comes from understanding the people who will use it.

I've worked with brands including Google, Apple, The Economist, Burberry, Adidas and Virgin Galactic. In 2019, I co-founded Paper Trails with my designer brother Jake—a SaaS platform that turns adventure data into wall art. He handles the visuals, I handle the tech.

Outside of work, I'm kept busy by my wife, two girls, and a Vizsla who thinks she's in charge. When I can salvage some spare time, I like to run, cycle, lift heavy things, and build furniture.

Plate 1

Enterprise Fintech · 2024

Cross-platform solution serving both web and mobile clients. Architecture and component system that powers the adviser experience across devices.

Plate 2

Paper Trails

Co-founded SaaS · 2019

Turn your adventure data into wall art. Built this SaaS from scratch with my brother Jake—I handle the tech, he handles the design. Full-stack ownership from Stripe payments to Strava and Komoot integrations. A product born from a shared love of maps and the outdoors.

Date: 2019–Present Dimensions: Full-stack SaaS Collection: Co-founded

Plate 3

Meshii WiFi

Web3 Mobile App · 2023

Blockchain-integrated mobile app for decentralized WiFi sharing. Led architecture decisions and deployment strategies. Integrated Polygon blockchain functionality for token rewards using Alchemy API. Mobile-first experience with serverless backend infrastructure.
