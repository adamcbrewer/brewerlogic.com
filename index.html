01. About Me

I started my career as an aircraft technician in the British Army, serving in the UK, Germany, and Iraq. After leaving the forces, I taught myself to code and haven't looked back since.

That military background gave me discipline, adaptability, and a knack for solving problems under pressure. These days, I channel that into building web applications that are as robust as they are user-friendly.

I've had the privilege of working with some incredible brands—Google, Apple, The Economist, Burberry—and co-founded my own SaaS, Paper Trails, with my designer brother.
